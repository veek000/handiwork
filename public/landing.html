Your Safety

Your safety is our priority

Your safety and security are our top priorities. Handiwork has implemented a number of robust measures to ensure a safe and secure experience for both homeowners and service providers.

  • Verified Artisans

    All professionals on our platform undergo a rigorous verification process. We confirm their identity, credentials, and work history to give you confidence in who you are hiring. You can see their ratings and reviews from other users before you book a job.

  • Secure Escrow System

    Our secure escrow system protects your payment. When you book a service, the funds are held securely until the job is completed to your satisfaction. Payment is only released to the professional after you confirm the work is done and you are happy with the results.

  • 24/7 Support

    Our support team is available around the clock to assist you with any issues or concerns you may have. If a dispute arises, we are here to help mediate and ensure a fair resolution for everyone involved.

  • Ratings & Reviews

    After every completed job, both parties are encouraged to leave a rating and review. This system helps maintain a high standard of quality and accountability on our platform, creating a trustworthy community for everyone.
