Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Handiwork ("Handiwork," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our platform to connect as a Customer or a Vendor for local services.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Handiwork web application and any associated services. It does not apply to third-party sites you may reach through links on the platform (e.g. a vendor's external portfolio).

2. Information We Collect

Account information: name, email address, phone number, password (stored hashed, never in plain text), and account role (Customer or Vendor).

Vendor-specific information (if you register as a Vendor): service listings, pricing, bio, years of experience, and availability, which are displayed publicly to Customers.

Job and communication data: job/service requests, order status, chat messages exchanged between Customer and Vendor within the platform, and reviews/ratings you submit or receive.

Usage and device data: log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) collected automatically for security and reliability purposes.

Payment and transaction information: job/payment amount, service fee, processing fee, escrow status, and Vendor payout account details (for settlement purposes). We do not collect or store your card or bank credentials directly — these are entered and held by Flutterwave. See Section 5.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to: operate and maintain your account; connect Customers with Vendors for requested services; enable in-app communication between Customer and Vendor for a given job; display Vendor profiles, listings, and reviews; send account-related notifications (e.g. a welcome email on signup); and maintain platform security and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on the following third-party providers to operate the platform. Each processes data only as needed to provide their service to us:

  • Convex — database and backend infrastructure; stores account, job, and message data.
  • Convex Auth — authentication; manages login credentials and sessions.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (e.g. welcome emails).
  • Flutterwave — payment processing and escrow; handles Customer payment and Vendor payout details directly. Flutterwave's own privacy policy governs how they process this data.

This list will be updated if additional providers are introduced.

5. Payments

Payments are processed by Flutterwave, a licensed third-party payment processor. Funds are held in escrow and released to the Vendor once a job is confirmed complete. Handiwork retains a service fee (currently 2%) from each completed transaction; a separate processing fee is passed through to Flutterwave at cost. Refund treatment (full refund vs. a retained cancellation fee) depends on the job stage and who cancelled — see the Terms of Use, Section 7, for the full policy. Handiwork does not directly collect or store your card or bank credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and job data for as long as your account is active. Chat messages are retained for the duration of the associated job and a limited period afterward for support purposes. You may request deletion at any time (Section 7).

7. Your Rights

If you are located in Nigeria, you have rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at [PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL].

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (e.g. hashed passwords, access controls via our infrastructure providers) to protect your information. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Handiwork is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Material changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date.
