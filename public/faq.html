Getting Started

Handiwork connects homeowners with verified local professionals for any home service, and lets you manage the whole job — quote, booking, and payment — in one place.

Almost any home service — carpentry, plumbing, painting, roofing, electrical work, cleaning, gardening, and more. Describe the job and trusted pros nearby send you quotes.

Open the app, describe the job, and submit your request. Providers in your area review it and send detailed quotes for you to compare.

Creating an account and requesting quotes is free. You only pay for the service you book, with funds held safely in escrow until the job is done.

Payments & Escrow

When you accept a quote, you deposit the funds into a secure escrow account. The money is only released to the provider after you confirm the job is complete and you're satisfied.

Payment is released from escrow the moment you mark the job complete and confirm your satisfaction — never before.

Major debit and credit cards and bank transfer. The exact options available depend on your region and are shown at checkout.

None. You see the provider's quote up front and approve the amount before any funds move into escrow — no surprise charges.

Safety & Trust

Every professional goes through a rigorous verification process: we confirm identity, credentials, and work history, and you can review ratings from other users before booking.

Because funds stay in escrow until you confirm, you're always in control. If something isn't right, our 24/7 support team helps mediate a fair resolution.

We verify each provider's credentials during onboarding, including applicable insurance and licensing where required for their trade, so you can book with confidence.

Report any concern directly in the app or to our support team, available around the clock. We take every report seriously and act quickly to keep the community safe.
